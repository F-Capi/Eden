Zornitser's research investigates themes of transience and liminality, with a particular emphasis on individuals emerging from local queer and underground scenes who challenge social norms. Their work examines spaces associated with queer culture, utilizing an analog large-format camera. By applying hormone liquids to chromogenic printing papers, Zornitser enhances the visual representation of transformative experiences, creating a dialogue between the medium and the subject.

Eden Zornitser (they/them) is a visual artist and photographer based in Tel Aviv, Israel-Palestine.

They graduated with honors from the Bezalel Academy of Arts and Design in Jerusalem, earning a Bachelor of Fine Arts degree in 2022.
